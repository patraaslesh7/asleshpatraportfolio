Research Publications Section

Utilizing VCG Auction Method for Quality Assurance and Efficient Task Allocation in Uniform Crowdsourcing Environment

IEEE Conference Publication December 2024

Patra A. (2024)

Here are the key points of this paper

  • Task Allocation and Quality Control: This paper addresses the challenges of effective task allocation and quality control in crowdsourcing environments with homogeneous workers.
  • VCG Auction Mechanism: The authors integrate the Vickrey-Clarke-Groves (VCG) auction mechanism with advanced computational optimization techniques to ensure truthful bidding and optimal task distribution.
  • Dynamic Task Assignment: The proposed framework dynamically adjusts task assignments based on real-time feedback and quality metrics, addressing both task allocation and quality control.
  • Robust Solution: The work provides a robust solution for optimizing task allocation and quality control in crowdsourcing platforms, offering significant improvements in efficiency and reliability.
